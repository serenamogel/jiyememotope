Born in Brooklyn, New York, Bruce Kulick showed an early interest in music. He started his professional career in the mid-1970s with the band Blackjack, alongside Michael Bolton. Although the band didn’t achieve significant commercial success, it laid the foundation for Kulick’s future in music.
