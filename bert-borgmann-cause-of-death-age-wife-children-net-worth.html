After difficulties from surgery, Bert Borgmann, a veteran CHSAA associate commissioner and a significant figure in Colorado prep sports passed away on Monday. He was 67.

From 1988 to 2021, Borgmann spent 33 years working for the association managing a number of sports.

His primary sports were basketball and baseball, but while serving as a CHSAA president, Borgmann also oversaw football, gymnastics, softball, soccer, music, student leadership, and the Hall of Fame.

Broomfield and Colorado State alumnus Borgmann spent more than three decades as a regular at high school sporting events in Colorado. He began his career at CHSAA as the director of business partnerships and media.

Borgmann became a “foundational pillar” while assisting in modernizing the association, according to former commissioner Rhonda Blanford-Green.
