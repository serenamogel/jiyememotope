8 Things You Didnt Know About Iman Ali

➡ Iman Ali Biography

One of the top-notched Pakistani stars Iman Ali, known for her excellent talent as she is a great actress and works in Urdu films. She always gives stellar performances that win the hearts of many. Her good looks and stylish appearance is enough to catch the attention of many. She made her debut in 2007 with a leading role in the thriller film named ‘Khuda Ke Liye’ for which she was honored with a Lux Style Award for the category of Best Actress. However, this does not end here as we are bringing you some unknown things you did not know.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Iman Ali

1- Multi-talented Star

Iman Ali is known for being a multi-talented star as she is not just an actress, yet a veteran model as well as she has done various modeling tasks.

2- As a Singer

The gorgeous beau Iman Ali is also known for being a great singer as she is blessed with a melodious voice that steals the hearts of many.

3- She & Social Media

Iman Ali is active on all her social media accounts where she puts a variety of stuff to amuse her fans and followers.

4- Association with the Industry

She has a strong association with the industry as her father Abid Ali (late) was also a veteran actor, and her two sisters also belong to the industry.

5- Recognition

She got much recognition after appearing in the thriller debut film ‘Khuda Ke Liye’ released in the year 2007. For the role, she got awarded a Lux Style Award for Best Actress.

6- Hobbies

Despite a busy schedule, Iman Ali always takes some time out for reading books and doing yoga asanas in her free time.

7- Controversy

The controversy sparked when a fatwa was issued against her for playing the role in the debut film ‘Khuda Ke Liye’.

8- Lovelife

The rumors speculated that she was in a relationship with a famed Indian director Imtiaz Ali. Later, she married to a Canadian businessman Babar Bhatti in the year 2019.
