Victoria Beckham Net Worth & Income

British singer, actress & philanthropist Victoria Beckham’s net worth is a staggering $450 million.

Though Victoria Beckham combines the whopping net worth with her husband, David Beckham, it still is a massive figure.

Her massive fortune has been greatly contributed by her successful music career, fashion & TV career.

Spice Girls Income

As mentioned before, Victoria rose to prominence as a member of the girl group Spice Girls.

Spice Girls has sold hundreds of millions of albums, earning a fortune to each member of the group.

However, her time with the group came to an end in 2000 as the group broke up and the members focused on their solo careers.

Nonetheless, by the time it had broken up, each member of the group had made a name and fame for themselves.

Victoria earned around $75 million annually during her time with the Spice Girls, and so did the other members, which seems justifiable referencing the albums they sold.

Nevertheless, seven years after the group broke up, they reunited for a tour in 2007.

Their tour began in December of 2007, a month after they released their Greatest Hits album.

The tour was named The Return of the Spice Girls, which played across Europe and North America.

It grossed over $70 million and also produced tickets and merchandise sales of $107.2 million, making it the 8th highest-grossing concert tour of 2008.

Similarly, the group sold out London’s The O2 Arena for 17 nights, making it the highest-grossing engagement of the year, drawing 256,647 fans and taking $33.8 million from a single venue.

Though Spice Girls has not been active in years, the members still earn well from music royalties.

Victoria Beckham | Houses, Cars, Private Jet & Yacht

Victoria Beckham is a true entrepreneur and has branched out in several industries, including real estate.

Victoria Beckham | Career

Victoria Beckham, originally Victoria Caroline Adams, was born on 17th April 1974 in Essex, England.

Her parents founded a successful electronics wholesale business.

She grew up wealthy along with her sister and brother. She decided to become a performer after she saw the film Fame in 1980.

Beckham studied modeling and dance at Jason Theater School and Laine Theater Arts School.

Unlike others born with a silver spoon, Beckham was embarrassed by her father’s wealth and begged not to drop her off in Rolls Royce.

In 1994 Victoria joined an all-female group along with Emma Bunton, Geri Halliwell, Melanie Chisholm, and Melanie Brown.

The girl group Spice Girls released the first single titled ‘Wannabe’ in 1996, which topped the chart in the UK, US, and 35 other countries.
