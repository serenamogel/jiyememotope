David Beador Welcomes First Child With Wife Lesley Cook

David Beador and his wife Lesley Cook welcomed their first child together on 6 February 2021, a daughter named Anna.

Lesley confirmed their daughter’s arrival news on 6 February, sharing a photo of the couple and their newborn on Instagram captioned with a selfie, 'Our first sunset with our sweet little Anna. (Pronounced Ah-na).'

David Beador and his wife Lesley Cook with their newborn child (©: Instagram/Lesley Beador)

The Real Housewives of Orange County star David Beador, 55, also shares three daughters with his ex-wife Shannon Beador - Sophie, 19, and twins Stella and Adeline, 16. On the other side, Lesley is also the mother of a daughter and son from the previous relationship.

Past few months, Lesley seemingly teased the pregnancy news on her private Instagram Story, sharing a photo of a literal bun in the oven and writing, 'You're just a small bump.' Apart from that, she told her daughter Inga in July 2021 that she had a sibling on the way. 'You’re gonna be a big sister, can you believe it?' Cook asked in a TikTok video at the time. 'Are you excited? Wow, that’s a lot of excitement.'

David and Lesley started dating in 2017 after first meeting at a SoulCycle class. During the time, David was still married to his former wife, Shannon Storms. After two years, he and Shannon finalized their divorce legally sharing their three children as 50/50 custody.

David then finally got engaged to his new ladylove Lesley on 31 January 2020. She announced their engagement news through Instagram by showing off engagement rings, 'Looking forward to eternity with my love. I LOVE YOU LOVE!!!'

After David and Lesley tied the knot on 15 October 2020, she changed her surname to 'Lesley Beador.' She also shared a screengrab of the milestone moment, adding the hashtag #LesleyBeador and tagging her location as "Dreams Come True."
