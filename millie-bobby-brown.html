Who is Millie Bobby Brown?

Have you ever watched Netflix's 'Stranger Things? If yes you are definitely known with the same Millie Bobby Brown who played the breakout role as a telekinetic child.

Millie Bobby Brown was born on 19 February 2004 in Marbella, Spain to the parent's Kelly and Robert Brown. Likewise, she is the fourth children of her parents. She holds English citizenship and ethnicity is mixed.

Concerning her education, she went to Pokesdown Community Primary School in England. Later, She attended weekend stage school. Her siblings are Paige Brown (Older Sister), Charlie Brown (Older Brother), Ava Brown (Younger Sister).

Brown started her early acting career as a role of Alice in ABC's Once Upon a Time in Wonderland. Soon after Bobby performed as Madison O'Donnell in BBC America's Intruders. Moreover, she also starred in smaller roles on shows like NCIS, Modern Family, and Grey’s Anatomy.

Millie's notable work in the medical series Stranger Things paved ways for her character as Eleven. This series gave her critical awards and acclaim like Screen Guild Award, and Saturn Award.
