What does the term 210t polyester refer to?

Denier is a unit of measurement for the weight in grammes of 9000 metres of yarn. The letter D stands for it. 210D Polyester, on the other hand, means that 9000 metres of polyester will weigh 210 grammes. As a result, 210T polyester will weigh 210 grammes per 9000 metres.

Pongee is a woven fabric with an irregular filling and a fine regular warp that is medium in weight and balanced. It used to refer to a fabric with irregular filling yarns and fine warp yarns, but now it refers to any fabric with irregular filling yarns and fine warp yarns.
