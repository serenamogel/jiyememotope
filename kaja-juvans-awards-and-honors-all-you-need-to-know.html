Kaja Juvans Awards And Honors: All You Need To Know

Kaja Juvan is a Slovenian professional tennis player. She achieved a career-high WTA singles ranking of world No. 58 on June 6 2022. She turned professional on October 17, 2016 and has participated in all tournaments. At the 2021 Winners Open in Cluj-Napoca, Juvan won her maiden WTA Tour doubles with her partner Natela Dzalamidze. She has won 7 ITF singles titles and 1 ITF title in doubles as well.

Did Kaja Juvan win a WTA Title?

Yes, Kaja Juvan has won WTA title in women’s doubles. She achieved that feat partnering with Natela Dzalamidze at the 2021 Winners Open in Cluj-Napoca.

Did Kaja Juvan win a AEGON Title?

Kaja Juvan has never won AEGON title in her professional career as of yet.

Did Kaja Juvan win ATP Title?

Kaja Juvan has so far not won any ATP Title.

Did Kaja Juvan win ITF World Champion?

Yes, Kaja Juvan has won the ITF World Champion. She has 7 ITF singles titles and 1 ITF doubles title to her name as of now.

How many Tournaments has Kaja Juvan won?

Kaja Juvan has won a few tournaments in her career thus far, including Orange Bowl in 2016, 2018 Summer Youth Olympics and 7 ITF titles in singles.

How many Medals does Kaja Juvan have?

Kaja Juvan has two Gold medals to her name. She won gold in doubles at the 2018 Summer Youth Olympics in Buenos Aires along with partner Iga Świątek of Poland.
