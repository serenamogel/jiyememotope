Who is Cam Reddish:

Cam Reddish is a famous Basketball Player. He was born on September 1, 1999 and his birthplace is United States. Cam is also well known as, Small forward who gained fame playing high school basketball for Westtown School before committing to play for Duke University. After one year at Duke, he declared for the NBA and he would go on to be selected 10th overall in the 2019 NBA Draft by the Atlanta Hawks. 

Cam is originated from United States. He was rated as a five-star recruit in 2018 along with fellow Duke commit Zion Williamson.
