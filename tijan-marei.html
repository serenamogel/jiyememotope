Tijan Marei- Professional Career

Tijan began her acting career at the age of eleven. She made her television debut in the film Das Echo der Schuld in 2009. She played the role of Nelly under the direction of Marcus O. Rosenmüller. Thanks to Bundesjugendspiele, she landed tiny roles in films and television shows such as Inga Lindström, Paare, Die Eifelpraxis, Maria, Argentinien und die Sache mit dem Weißwürsten, Siebenstein, Oblivio, Who Am I, Monkey King, Deus Ex Macchina, and others.

Her breakthrough role in her career came in the year (2017) when she played Ella in the TV movie Ellas Baby. This actor afterward participated in films such as Leipzig Homicide, Wishlist, Großstadtrevier, Notruf Hafenkante, and others in 2018. She rose to popularity in the Netflix film The Privilege, which she co-directed with Felix Fuchssteiner and Katharina Schöde, as Samira Shirvani. In one of the main parts, she co-starred with Max Schimmelpfennig as FInn Bergmann and Lea van Acken as Lena.
