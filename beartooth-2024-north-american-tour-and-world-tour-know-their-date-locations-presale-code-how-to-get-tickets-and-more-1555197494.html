Beartooth Band 

Beartooth is an American rock band founded by Caleb Shomo in 2012. What makes Beartooth unique is its origin as a one-man studio project, with Shomo taking on every instrument recording. This solo endeavor eventually evolved into a four-member band. The band's journey with Red Bull Records began in 2013, marked by the release of their debut EP, "Sick," in the same year. The full-length album "Disgusting" followed in 2014, signaling their arrival in the rock music scene.

Article continues below advertisementhttps://www.instagram.com/p/CyWLsyEiKSM/?img_index=1

Over the years, Beartooth has continued to make its mark with several studio albums, including "Aggressive" in 2016, "Disease" in 2018, and "Below" in 2021. Their fifth studio album, "The Surface," was released in October 2023, showcasing their enduring commitment to powerful, emotionally charged rock music. Beartooth's music serves as a cathartic outlet for themes like suicidal ideation, emotional turmoil, and anxiety, resonating with a dedicated fan base.

Their impact in the industry is evident through their RIAA-certified gold single, invitations to major festivals, and tours with renowned acts like Slipknot, Bring Me The Horizon, and A Day To Remember.

Beartooth Songs

One of their most notable achievements was earning RIAA Gold certification for "In Between," a song that struck a chord with listeners.
