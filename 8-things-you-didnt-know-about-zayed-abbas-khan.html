8 Things You Didnt Know About Zayed Abbas Khan

One of the most acclaimed Indian actor, Zayed Abbas Khan, who is known for his acting skills as initially, he gave brilliant performances in many Bollywood films, later he turned his passion in the production line and produced few films. In 2003, he made his debut film Chura Liya Hai Tumne in which he shared the screen space along with Esha Deol. While he worked on a Bollywood film Love Breakups Zindagi as a producer as it was his first produced film which was released in the year 2011. Meanwhile, he gave a few appearances in other Bollywood films including ‘Mein Hoon Naa as a supporting actor alongside Shahrukh Khan, Sushmita Sen as lead actors, while Amrita Arora appeared in a supporting role. Well, this does not end here as we have compiled some of his lesser facts to know about him. Let us have a look!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Zayed Abbas Khan

Zayed Abbas Khan is a man who believes in productivity and he loves spending his free time playing adventure sports, biking, and playing the guitar which he calls his first love.

2- Dating/Married Life

There was news circulating that Zayed Khan and Esha Deol had a long-term relationship as they dated for a long, but later, they denied and said, they are just good friends. While Khan married Malaika Parekh in the year 2005. The couple is blessed with two sons named Zidaan Khan and Aariz Khan.

Zayed Abbas Khan is the son of Sanjay Khan who had worked as a renowned actor in the Hindi cinema and also worked as a filmmaker, while his mother Zarine Khatrak Khan is a famed Interior Designer.

Zayed Khan is a foodie person and loves eating Red Masala Chops, Kalmiri Ka Achar, Bangalore Daal, Besan Laddoo. It seems he is fond of eating a variety of food.

Among many top-celebrated actors, Zayed Abbas Khan adores his most favorite Bollywood stars including Hrithik Roshan and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan for their admiring acting skills.

Zayed Khan was an active child during his school days as he used to participate in his school events and also performed in English theatre plays to hone his acting skills.

The skinny, charming actor, Khan is a well-trained filmmaker as he learned filmmaking from the London Film Academy. Undoubtedly, he has a good command over the production line and everyone appreciates him.
