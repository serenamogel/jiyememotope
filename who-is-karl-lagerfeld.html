Where was Karl Lagerfeld Born?

Lagerfeld was born to Elisabeth (née Bahlmann) and Otto Lagerfeld, a businessman, on September 10, 1933, in Hamburg. His maternal grandfather, Karl Bahlmann, was a local politician for the Catholic Centre Party, and his father owned a business that produced and imported evaporated milk.

Lagerfeld frequently lied about his age and family history, even saying he was born in a different year. He said his parents were “Elisabeth of Germany” and “Otto Ludwig Lagerfeldt” from Sweden, and that he was born in 1938. These rumors have been debunked once and for all by the fact that his father was born and raised in Germany, not Sweden.

Elisabeth Bahlmann, his mother, was the daughter of a middle-class local politician, but there is no evidence that she ever identified as “Elisabeth of Germany.” He always claimed that nobody knew his actual birthday. Lagerfeld said he was born “neither in 1933 nor 1938” during a television interview in France in February 2009.
