Biography

Samuel García was born in Monterrey, Mexico on December 28, 1987. Mexican politician who is known for being a senator for the Congress of the Union of Mexico. He is also known for having been the Deputy to the Congress of the State of Nuevo Leon. He is a part of the Citizen’s Movement Party. Other Senators also from Mexico include Laura Angélica Rojas Hernández and María Rojo. He completed his studies from the Tecnologico de Monterrey University where he earned his bachelor in Law and Finance. He went on to obtain his Masters in Public Law in 2012 and later his Doctorate in Public Policy and Administration.
