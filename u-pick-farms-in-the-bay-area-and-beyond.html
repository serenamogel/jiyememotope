U-Pick Farms in the Bay Area and Beyond

Who doesn't love fresh fruit and veggies? Visiting a U-Pick farm is a fun experience for the whole family, plus you end up with some yummy goodies to enjoy! In addition to offering u-pick fruits, many farms also offer pre-picked fruits and vegetables. There are some great U-Pick farms in the Bay Area and we've rounded them up for you all in one place. Get out and pick your own fruit in the Bay Area and beyond and don't forget your camera!

Visit each farm's website and/or give them a call to see what is currently available before planning your visit.
