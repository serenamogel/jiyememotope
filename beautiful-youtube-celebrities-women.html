With her charming personality and expertise in all things beauty, Zoella has become a role model for aspiring beauty vloggers around the world. She shares her knowledge and tips on makeup, skincare, and fashion, inspiring her viewers to embrace their unique style and find confidence in their own skin.

Not only is Zoella a talented content creator, but she has also ventured into various business ventures. She has launched her own beauty and lifestyle products, including a successful range of beauty products that has gained immense popularity.

Zoella’s honest and relatable approach to beauty and life has garnered her a loyal fanbase and made her one of the most influential English YouTubers in the industry. Her impact on the beauty community is undeniable, and her success story continues to inspire many aspiring beauty vloggers to pursue their passion on YouTube.

    Lilly Singh – The Late-Night Host

    Lilly Singh, also known as Superwoman, is a Canadian actress, comedian, and YouTuber. With her infectious humor and charismatic personality, she quickly rose to fame through her comedic videos on YouTube.

    However, Lilly didn’t stop there. She made history by becoming the first woman of Indian and South Asian descent to host a late-night talk show on a major network. Her show, “A Little Late with Lilly Singh,” premiered in 2019 on NBC.

    Lilly’s unique blend of wit, relatable storytelling, and celebrity interviews has resonated with audiences all over the world. As a late-night show host, she brings a fresh and diverse perspective to the industry, reflecting the changing landscape of entertainment.
